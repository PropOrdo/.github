## What is PropOrdo?
PropOrdo simplifies property and strata management by bringing governance into one smart, easy-to-use platform. From inspections and maintenance workflows to compliance, communication, and reporting, PropOrdo helps committees and property managers stay organised, accountable, and in control—without the spreadsheets, emails, or confusion. Whether you manage one building or many, PropOrdo turns complex property operations into clear, streamlined workflows.

Join the PropOrdo family today and turn your property into a well-oiled, eco-friendly machine. Smarter governance starts here!
